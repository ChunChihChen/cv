    Hello, my name is Elvis (é™³ä¿Šæ™º) and I am honored to introduce myself as a researcher 
    and Assistant Professor in the field of machine learning, circular economy, energy economy, and sustainability 
    at the National Taichung University of Science and Technology in Taiwan. 
    My passion for this field is evident in my numerous publications in reputable journals.
    
    In 2020, I earned a PhD from the Management Science Department at National Yang Ming Chiao Tung University. 
    During my studies, I had the opportunity to gain advanced knowledge and skills as a visiting scholar at North Carolina State University, 
    where I specialized in data analysis, machine learning, artificial intelligence, and heuristic algorithms.

    One notable achievement was the development of the Artificial Intelligent Manufacturing Parameter Process System (AIMPPS) for a car steering wheel manufacturing company,

    I am a highly analytical and resourceful problem-solver who is able to extract valuable insights from data to inform decision-making and problem-solving. 
    This makes me an adept collaborator and a valuable asset to any project. My recently funded project, 
    which aims to simulate the dynamic relationship between the EU's circular economy, energy consumption, environmental sustainability, 
    and economic growth using deep learning methods, received funding from the National Science and Technology Council. 
    I am always looking for opportunities to learn and grow and welcome collaborations with colleagues from diverse backgrounds and perspectives, 
    as this enhances my ability to work as part of a team and achieve common goals.
    
    In addition to my professional achievements, I bring a good sense of humor and a passion for sports to any project. 
    I am open to discussing potential collaborations or any other relevant topics and look forward to connecting with you.
