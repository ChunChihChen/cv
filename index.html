🤘 Hello 🤘

Hi, I am Elvis Chen(陳俊智). I am an assistant professor in Department of International Business, National Taichung University of Science and Technology, Taiwan.
My research interests are circular economy, energy economy/policy, and sustainability.
I obtained PhD from Dept. of Management Science, NYCUin 2020.
My accomplishments during doctoral trajectory includes publishing 3 research field related papers on well-known journals.

I was nominated, and awarded a two-year fellowship by the Ministry of Education of Taiwan and visited North Carolina State University in Raleigh, NC as a visiting scholar.
I also participated in a conference(2nd International Conference on Energy Research and Social Science)in Arizona State University.
This precious opportunity broadened my horizon via knowing more about different cultures and lifestyles.

Currently, I am an assistant professor in Department of International Business, National Taichung University of Science and Technology.
If you are looking for a competent researcher, who also has a good sense of humor to collaborate on circular economy, sustainability and data mining, please feel free to send me the message.
I am an open-minded person, and I accept various opinions on widely discussed topics, I can cooperate with different researchers no problem.
If you really want to, we could also discuss sport related stuff. I am a sports enthusiast, feel free to chat with me about it in our free time :D
